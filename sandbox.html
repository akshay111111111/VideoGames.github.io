What is a sandbox game?
A sandbox game is a genre of a game in which players are given the ability to accomplish an objective trough creative methods and tools which makes it so that sandbox games can be fun for everyone as you can do what you want in a way you want to do it in fact some sandbox games don't even have objectives and are termed software toys

One of the most underrated sandbox-style videogame franchise of all time is the hitman series.This series follows you the player playing as agent 47 working for a organisation hunting down targets across the globe.it's James Bond like style really influences you to dig deeper into the game and it also gives you various different ways to eliminate your target you can go for a sneak attack or a full force attack causing the player to play the game in a way they want to play.
